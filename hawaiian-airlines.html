Hawaiian Airlines Wedding Wings Interface

What do you do when you are a huge airline that wants to cater to a small demographic, bring in the lowly user experience designer I suppose. Hawaiian Airlines needed an interface that strictly matched their brand guidelines and allowed couples flying to Hawaii to tie the knot, the ability to invite their friends and family to save on airfare. I relished the chance to design a fresh take on their brand guidelines and get my hands dirty coding the front end in HTML, CSS, and jQuery.

It all starts with a "Plane"

This project had a large number of user needs, since I worked so closely with the client and strictly followed their brand guidelines, most of the work was spent mapping out the user experience and flow of the interface. Countless hours were spent on the whiteboard creating and recreating wireframes, but I'm into that sort of thing.
