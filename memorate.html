Memorate UI & UX

Memorate.com is an amazing new social network that empowers people to collaborate and share in each other's stories, by co-authoring stories with friends and family. They needed a fresh new look that would appeal to their target demographic while still staying relevant to all markets. I did a complete overhaul on there User Flow and Strategy while designing them a simple and stylized interface with and uncluttered and classic feel.

Worked and re-worked

This project had a large number of user needs, on top of the complete interface overhaul I helped them completely re-work the user flow of their system. Most of the work was spent mapping out the user experience and flow of the interface. Countless hours were spent on the whiteboard creating and recreating wireframes, but I'm into that sort of thing.
